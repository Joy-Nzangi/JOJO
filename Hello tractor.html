Hello Tractor hosted a UI/UX Design Hackathon focused on redesigning their Tractor Operator App‚Äîa tablet-first tool used by tractor operators across Africa to manage jobs, farms, and their journey toward tractor ownership.


I participated as a UI/UX & Product Designer, applying user research and product thinking to create a clearer, more motivating operator experience. My solution won 3rd place.


Problem

Tractor operators work in time-sensitive, outdoor environments where usability matters more than aesthetics alone.

The challenge was to:

  • Improve usability on tablet screens
  • Reduce friction in daily job management
  • Make tractor ownership progress visible and motivating
  • Balance utility + delight, inspired by Tesla & BMW apps

Goal: Design a secure, accessible, and easy-to-use platform for the African tractor marketplace that bridges farmers, sellers, and operators.


Target Users

  • Tractor Operators managing multiple daily jobs
  • Booking Agentsonboarding farms and creating service requests
  • Operators working toward tractor ownership

Research & Insights

Given the hackathon format, I conducted rapid, lightweight research to ground design decisions:

1. Product & Domain Review
  • Reviewed Hello Tractor‚Äôs existing operator workflows and feature set
  • Studied the end-to-end operator journey: job requests ‚Üí execution ‚Üí payments ‚Üí ownership

2. Comparative UX Analysis

Analyzed Tesla and BMW apps for:

  • Quick-access controls
  • Clear visual hierarchy
  • Minimal cognitive load

Key takeaway: great utility tools feel calm, confident, and predictable


Key Insights
  • Operators need to understand their day at a glance
  • Maps and farm boundaries must remove uncertainty
  • Progress toward ownership should feel rewarding, not abstract

Design Goals

  • Tablet-first, mobile-ready experience
  • Big, touch-friendly components
  • Clear prioritization of tasks
  • Interactive, easy-to-read maps
  • Gamified progress toward tractor ownership

My Approach

I treated the challenge like a product redesign, not a visual refresh:

  • Simplified user flows for job management
  • Designed dashboards that support fast decision-making
  • Used motion and progress indicators to reinforce motivation
  • Ensured scalability across tablet and mobile

Key Design Solutions

  • Daily Job Overview: A single dashboard showing jobs, priorities, and optimized routes.
  • Interactive Farm Maps: Zoomable farm polygons with clear boundaries and spatial clarity.
  • Quick Job Actions: Start, end, reschedule, or cancel jobs with minimal taps.
  • Booking Agent Mode: Enables operators to onboard farms and create service bookings.
  • Tractor Ownership Tracker: A milestone-driven progress system that turns effort into visible achieveme
