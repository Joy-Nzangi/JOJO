Kalimani

An All-in-One Platform to Learn and Teach Kenyan Vernacular Languages

Scroll

What if Kenyan youth could learn their vernacular languages in a fun, structured, and supported way?

Product Kalimani
Role Product Design & Lead
Sector EdTech | Language Learning
Impact Expanded access to Kenyan vernacular learning & teaching

Kalimani is a digital learning platform designed to help Kenyans & non Kenyans learn Kenyan vernacular languages while empowering teachers to teach, earn, and scale their impact. The platform addresses the growing gap between language heritage and modern learning habits.


Problem

Kalimani was born out of a clear problem: Kenyan youth want to learn their languages, but current solutions are fragmented, inaccessible, and uninspiring. At the same time, teachers have the skills but lack platforms to reach learners and earn sustainably. This project explores how we designed a learning platform that bridges this gap.


Discovery & Research

I started by interviewing learners and tutors to understand real motivations and blockers.
Key insights from learners:

  • Willing to pay for a structured learning experience
  • Need fun, bite‑sized lessons that fit into daily life
  • Tutor support is critical when stuck during learning


Key insights from tutors:

  • Have teaching expertise but lack distribution channels
  • Need support with content creation, marketing, and payments
  • Want predictable income and professional growth


These insights shaped our opportunity: Build a learning platform that supports learners with structure and tutors with tools.


Approach

Our strategy focused on three pillars:

1) Learner Engagement

We crafted short, progressive lessons that:

  • Are easy to complete daily
  • Reinforce culture through stories and real expressions
  • Include optional tutor Q&A support
Teacher Enablement

We built features for teachers to:

  • Upload and manage their own lessons
  • Host live or recorded sessions
  • Handle bookings and payments seamlessly
3) Accessible Growth

We prioritized mobile‑first experiences and social media hooks for organic reach — especially on platforms like TikTok where language content already gains traction.


Solution

Structured Learning Paths

Learners follow curated progressions from beginner to advanced fluency with clear milestones, quiz checks, and cultural context.

Tutor Hub

Teachers can:

  • Create & track sharable lesson videos
  • Manage student bookings
  • Track earnings and ratings

Social & Community Features

Learners and teachers can interact, share progress, and participate in language challenges.


Go-to-Market & Business Model

  • Pilot: Kikuyu classes with school & media partner
  • Organic traction: TikTok content drawing learners locally & in the diaspora
  • Revenue share: 50% Learning Center, 25% Media Partner, 25% Kalimani, transitioning to student subscriptions

Impact & Reflection

  • Expanded access to vernacular learning & teaching
  • Supported cultural preservation
  • Learned the importance of designing for both learners and educators

Next Steps: Expand to more languages, launch subscriptions, add AI tools for teachers.
